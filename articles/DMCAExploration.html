Exploration of the Digital Millennium Copyright Act

When I took CSCI: 409 Cyberscurity Law and Policy at NDSU, I had to choose from a selection of laws to write a final paper on. I selected the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) due to its influence on my personal life. As highlighted in this paper, my friends have had a few "run-ins" with this law and I wanted to do more research into it.

Many thanks to Professor Rabadi for the opportunity to research this law. Without this opportunity, I wouldn't have discovered the major flaws within the DMCA.
